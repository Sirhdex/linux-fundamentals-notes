## Concepts
- Regular Expressions allow us to find, replace, and manipulate with extreme percision
- Symbols and Characters put together to form a search pattern
- Grouping regular expressions together is also possible

## Commands
- (a) -- regular expression -- group of regex processed together
- [a-z] -- regular expression -- defines character classes; you specify the list
- {1,10} -- regular expression -- define quantifiers
- | -- regular expression -- OR operator
- .* -- regular expression -- AND operator (kind of, only when both expressions are true)

## Examples
- grep -E -- lets us utilize regex
